Important information regarding widgets:
Price includes half year support for future bug fixing and small tweaking updates.
Price does not include big changes to design after agreement on final design and big changes to code after the project has finalized.
Any widget functionality can be customized and mixed. You can propose features not listed here and I will try to implement them as long as it is possible via StreamElements.
If you request specific animations I will try my hardest to satisfy your vision, even though I am not experienced in animation.
All widgets are for twitch since I have not yet made anything for youtube. If you are willing to wait a bit more for a youtube or multistream version, feel free to contact me!
Additionally, all widgets are for StreamElements only, because this platform has the most features and support for customizability. Though I am open to other similar tools!
Custom widgets outside of the listed ones will be considered and priced separately, please contact me to discuss it!
